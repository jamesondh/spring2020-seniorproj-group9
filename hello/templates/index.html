What does this do?

Twitter Sentiment Analysis is a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform for user-friendly and cost-effective sentiment analysis of social media data on Twitter.

Our platform automatically scrapes Twitter and runs a sentiment analysis algorithm to determine on a scale of -10 to 10 how negatively or positively people are talking using a given keyword.

Who is this for?

This service can be used by any non-technical or technical person interested in easily exploring sociological data, including but not limited to:

  • Researchers
  • Customer analysts
  • Social media managers
  • Investors

How does it work?

  1. Go to your dashboard and the Create Job button.
  2. Enter a hashtag you want to analyze for sentiment.
  3. Wait a few minutes and return to your dashboard to see the results.
